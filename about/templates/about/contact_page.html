Ozford is located in the heart of Melbourne. You can find us at 310 King Street in the Melbourne CBD, close to Flagstaff Garden. At Ozford, you will experience the benefits and advantages of a city campus in a multicultural, bustling business district.

Our campus has three levels with modern facilities. Recently renovated with an open plan design, our Ground Floor is the perfect place to study. The brand new library/student resource hub is open Monday to Friday 8.30am – 5pm with new technology and an impressive selection of books to borrow. The library has a wonderful view of Flagstaff gardens and voted the best spot to relax and study by many Ozford students. Our friendly student support staff, accounts department and international marketing team are all located on the Ground floor where students can ask for help with academic support and guidance.

Ozford Institute of Higher Education is located in a perfect spot, positioned between some of Melbourne’s best restaurants, popular tourist attractions and Victoria’s main train stations. The Olympic Park is only a 30 minute walk which is where main sporting events are held all year round. The Marvel Stadium is even closer: the home of AFL matches. The location of Ozford Institute helps students become familiar with Melbourne city and their surroundings, like getting public transport and learning their commute. In busy cities like Melbourne, there are fun events organised every day of the week, especially for international students. Many Ozford students met their friends at student events and highly recommend attending some, which are held at venues nearby.
