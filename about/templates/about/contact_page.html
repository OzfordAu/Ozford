Ozford is located in the heart of Melbourne’s vibrant CBD at 333 Queen Street, Melbourne, VIC 3000, just moments away from Flagstaff Gardens and surrounded by the energy of a bustling multicultural business district.

At Ozford, students benefit from all the advantages of a city campus — easy access to public transport, nearby cafés and restaurants, and the dynamic lifestyle that Melbourne is known for.

Our recently renovated campus offers modern facilities designed to help students succeed in their studies. The library and student resource hub is open Monday to Friday, 8:30am – 5:00pm, equipped with up-to-date technology and a wide range of books to borrow. With comfortable study spaces and views of nearby city landmarks, it’s a favourite spot for many Ozford students to read, relax and connect.

Our dedicated student support team, accounts department, and international marketing staff are always ready to help with academic guidance, student services and any questions you may have.

Ozford’s central location places students within walking distance of Melbourne’s major train stations, top restaurants, cultural attractions, and sporting venues like the Marvel Stadium and Olympic Park. Melbourne’s busy events calendar means there’s always something happening — from international student meetups to local festivals — providing plenty of opportunities to make friends and enjoy life in Australia’s most liveable city.
